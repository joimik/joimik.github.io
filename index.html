Key Relationships

Speed of light
c = λ · f
c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s  ·  λ = wavelength  ·  f = frequency
Photon energy (Planck)
E = h · f = hc / λ
h = 6.626 × 10⁻³⁴ J·s (Planck's constant)
Wave equation
E(x,t) = E₀ sin(kx − ωt)
k = 2π/λ  ·  ω = 2πf  ·  perpendicular B field oscillates in phase
